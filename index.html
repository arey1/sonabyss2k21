PHOTOGRAPHY CLUB (OPEN EVENT)

Photography club, SONABYSS 2K21-22 aims at the upbringing of creativity of the person behind the lens. In this SONABYSS join us in the journey to find the best artist with a sensor and lens.No matter professional photographer or amateur photography enthusiast we welcome all with our open arms.

1.Landscape photography

Landscape photography is the art of capturing pictures of nature and the outdoors in a way that brings your viewer into the scene. From grand landscapes to intimate details, the best photos demonstrate the photographer’s own connection to nature and capture the essence of the world around them.

2.Macro photography

Macro photography is the art of capturing the finest details in surrounding. From small insects to water droplet on grass anything can be captured under macro lens.

Portrait photography

A photo becomes a portrait when the subject along with the background, lighting and emotion can evoke a sense of connection between the viewer and the portrait photograph. A candid photo is not considered a portrait, however, the most candid portraits are often the best.

4.Spot photography

For spot photography 10 participants will be selected based on the photos submitted on above mentioned categories. Participants will have to be present on the selected place at given time for the contest. Date, place and time will be mentioned later. There will be two winners for this category.

5.Light photography

A ‘light painting’ is an image that is created by capturing a long exposure of a dark scene that contains a moving light source.Lower your shutter speed raise your imagination create the masterpiece.

6.Model photography

For model photography 10 participants will be selected based on the photos submitted on above mentioned categories. Participants will have to be present on the selected place at given time for the contest. Date, place and time will be mentioned later. There will be two winners for this category.

7.Wabi-Sabi photography

“Wabi-sabi” is a Japanese term that essentially means finding beauty in imperfection. Wabi refers to the kind of beauty found in asymmetrical, uneven or unbalanced things.Sabi is the beauty of aged things and speaks to the impermanence of life through the passage of time. The participants will have to demonstrate “wabi-sabi” in the best manner through photography.

QUIZ(Open Event)

Guidelines-
For Group events-Each team must have only two participants.
For Solo events- There will be independent individual participant.
—It will be a general quiz competition with a wide range of topics.
—There will be seven rounds under group event namely-
1. Rapid fire Round
2. Buzzer Round
3. Trivia Round
4. Abbreviation Round
5. Current Affairs Round
6. Sports Round
7. Choose the category Round

—There will be two different events under Solo events namely-\
1.Sudoku
2. Street quiz.

General Rules:-
(*)No. Of Questions asked, marking scheme of each rounds and other rules will be disclosed during event only.
(*)If there is a tie, the teams with equal marks gets into Trivia round to create difference in marks.
(*)In case of any dispute, the decision taken by the Quiz master or the Quiz Organisers shall be considered final.
(*)No electronic gadgets or equipments are allowed during the event.
(*)Any kind of written or printed material will not be allowed.
(*)Any team found using unfair means shall be immediately disqualified.
(*)Organisers hold the right to make changes in the event structure during event.

Rapid Fire Round:—
—In this round, each team will be certain number of question one after another.
—On the immediate completion of a question, each team will get 5 sec to answer it.
—There will be no repetition of questions and no team discussion.

Buzzer Round:—
—Questions will be fired at all the teams one after another. The team that presses the buzzer/bell first gets a chance to answer it.
— If team doesn’t answer or give wrong answer after pressing the buzzer, they will be lose points.

Trivia Round:—
—Questions will be fired at all the teams one after another. Each question have four options A,B,C & D from which one will be correct.

Abbreviation Round:—
Questions in this round will have abbreviations and acronyms.

Current Affairs Round:—
In this round, all the questions will be from current affairs, covering all topics.

Sports Round:—
In this round, each team will be asked questions from sports section.

Choose the Category Round:—
— In this round, each team will get to choice to choose any topics from the given topics and each team has its own quota of 4 questions from chosen topics.

**Other rules and regulations, if any, will be discussed at the venue on event day.

SONABYSS 2K21

On the unification of scores of like-minded and bright people coming from different parts of the unique eight sister states and students from all over India, some of whom are misfits, some the rebels, some of them are the round pegs in square holes, and all of them unitedly passionate about technological advancements in the society, and they all being the people who see things differently. This leads to the vector resultant in the form of a festive – a cultural festive of a collectanea of scintillating events having the power to hook anyone’s mind and soul and making the perceive seventh heaven none other than here, at the North Eastern Regional Institute of Science and Technology.
This cultural festive, is famously regarded as the SONABYSS.
SONABYSS – the very word that symbolizes the magnanimous and multifaceted extravaganza associated with our alma mater NERIST. It is an event that fills the heart of every NERISTIAN and other institutes with a bottomless abyss of expectation, and the fun-filled 2 days and 3 nights of song, dance, drama, exhibition, quiz, and many more, is the most eagerly awaited period by all students as well as other people all around. On account of being the biggest and the most spectacular cultural festival of Arunachal Pradesh, it provides a niche for the students at other colleges as well, to showcase their talents through a host of events which has become synonymous with this ultimate showdown.
Innovated in the year 1987, with a vision to nurture creativity and provide recreation to the students of NERIST, the Recreational & Creative Activities Forum (RACAF) has logged a long journey of 34 glorious years of its presence.This SONABYSS'21 is going to organize in newly inaugurated silver jubilee hall for the first time in NERIST. The stage will be surrounded by stalls of different sponsors as well as exhibitions from different clubs.
The exquisite and exponentially energetic SONABYSS has advanced a long way over these years. It has transcended every echelon of time and has become one of the major events organized by an exceptional fraternity of the students of NERIST. So, have you ever wondered what it would be like, had you just been a pawn in the game of life? – Where you are compelled to confront antagonizing situations which happen to be beyond the maxima of your control, and you, you my friend are provoked to survive. Hence, you endeavour to transcend and ameliorate the frontiers of your limits so as to amplify your abilities of survival. We are filled with immense zeal and are vociferously proud to annunciate that, this SONABYSS’21 is installed with the audacious theme of the “Squid Games”, hinting at the supremely adventurous events waiting to be unfurled and presented in front of all.
